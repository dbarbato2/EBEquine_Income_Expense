Powerful Features, Simple Interface

Designed for equine professionals who want to spend less time on paperwork and more time with their horses.

💰

Revenue Tracking

Log every service, lesson, and sale. Search by client, date, or invoice number. Generate professional invoices instantly.

🧾

Expense Management

Record vendor payments, supplies, and business trips. Full edit and delete capabilities with fuzzy search to find any record.

📋

Tax Deductions

Track mileage, tolls, insurance, utilities, and more. Generate a complete monthly deduction PDF for your accountant.

🐴

Client & Horse Records

Maintain full profiles for every client and horse — breed, age, medications, prior injuries, discipline, and contact info.

📊

Visual Dashboard

Charts and graphs for revenue trends, quarterly breakdowns, expense categories, and rolling totals. Export as PNG or CSV.

📄

Invoice Generator

Create fully editable, professional invoices with your logo. Print or save as HTML. Client details auto-populate from your records.

🔒

Secure Authentication

Protected with JWT-based login, encrypted passwords, and secure password reset via email. Your data stays private.

🔗

Google Forms Integration

New client intake forms automatically sync to the database via Google Sheets webhook — zero manual data entry.

🌙

Light & Dark Mode

Switch between light and dark themes to suit your preference. Settings are saved between sessions automatically.

How It Works

Get up and running in minutes with a straightforward workflow built around how your business actually operates.

1

Create Your Account

Register with your name and email. Your data is securely stored and tied to your account — no one else can see it.

2

Add Your Clients & Horses

Enter client profiles manually or let the Google Form intake webhook handle it automatically when new clients sign up.

3

Log Revenue, Expenses & Deductions

Record transactions as they happen. Each entry is timestamped, numbered, and instantly searchable.

4

Generate Reports & Invoices

Create a professional invoice from any revenue record in seconds. Download monthly tax reports as PDFs for your accountant.

5

Analyze Your Business

Use the dashboard to spot trends, compare quarters, and understand where your revenue is coming from — and where it's going.
